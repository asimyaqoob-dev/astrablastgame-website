About the Developer

Asim Yaqoob

I'm an engineer who loves to build things. I love nature, art and physics. I'm also a lifelong gamer who has always been fascinated by the creative process behind video games. Video games to me are the ultimate intersection of engineering with the arts. It's been a dream of mine to create and release my own game and I started the journey in 2021 with AstraBlast.

Making AstraBlast

I started to teach myself game development as a hobby in my spare time. It quickly grew into a labour of love and it's been one of the most enjoyable things I've ever done! AstraBlast is the result. It is small mobile indie game but a lot of love, time and effort went into creating it. I have plans to expand the game with new content and continuous updates. I will also be working on an entirely new game in a different genre! I hope you enjoy playing AstraBlast, and please let me know what you think by leaving a review on the App Store or Google Play Store. Thanks!

Shout Outs!
