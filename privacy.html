Section 2. Data Collected

The app collects in-game progression data using the Unity Analytics Service, which enables the developer; Asim Yaqoob, to understand and improve player experience. Data cannot be traced to individual identities nor is it shared with any external third parties outside of Unity.

The types of in-game data that are collected include:

  1. Player scores and skill point statistics
  2. Game over statistics
  3. Usage of unlockable assets

In addition to in-game data, additional data types are also collected:

  1. Coarse location (country that the device is located in)
  2. Device Model
  3. User and Device identifiers that are game-specific, which cannot be traced to individual identity.
