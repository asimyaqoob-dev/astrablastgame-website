The Mission

Blast off and steer your spacecraft to slingshot from one orbit to the next, avoiding obstacles, earning skill points and unlocking special powers along the way! How far into the galaxy can you blast?

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Blast off and slingshot your way from one orbit to the next!
  • Avoid obstacles including comets, moons and black holes!
  • Earn skill points along the way as you hone your skills!
  • Unlock power-ups that boost your abilities!
  • Earn revives to resurrect and continue your journey!

Features

  • Fun and endless "one more time" gameplay!
  • Simple tap and tilt controls!
  • Original music score by __MING__!
  • Over 40 distinct planets to discover along the way!
  • Cool power-ups including shields and time dilation!
  • Futuristic HUD and SFX that match your increasing speed!
  • Beautiful and seamless user interface!
